# About

**InnerShell Labs** is an independent security research space authored by **Diego Concha**.

This site focuses on security engineering and adversarial analysis of complex systems, with emphasis on:

- Security reviews and architecture-level risk analysis  
- Adversary emulation and Red Team perspectives  
- Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI) and real-world incident analysis  
- Malware behavior and execution flow analysis  

## Principles

- Depth over volume  
- Fundamentals over hype  
- Systems thinking over checklists
